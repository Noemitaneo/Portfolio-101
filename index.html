About Me

I am your web designer from the sunny Philippines! With a passion for crafting compelling online experiences, I am your go-to expert for web design, funnel building, and automation. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology and started my career as a front-end developer. Realizing coding wasn't for me, I transitioned to design and discovered GoHighLevel, which I fell in love with.

Now, I specialize in boosting campaign performance through clever automations, utilizing GoHighLevel and other CRM systems to ensure your digital journey is smooth and effective. I create and build campaigns for various clients across different industries, including coaching, consultation, webinars, and cart funnels. Every day, I embrace new technologies and continually learn and adapt, which excites me about my work. From creating captivating landing pages to orchestrating seamless workflows, you can trust my expertise to elevate your business. Let's build your dream funnel together with a smile!
